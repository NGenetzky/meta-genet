### Shell environment set up for builds. ###

This environment can be summarized as:
1. Some magical env variables, such as:
    - BBPATH
    - BUILDDIR
    - BB_ENV_EXTRAWHITE
2. Some standard env variables, such as: 
    - PATH
3. Some local configuration, such as:
    - ${BUILDDIR}/conf/local.conf
    - ${BUILDDIR}/conf/bblayers.conf
